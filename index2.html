How to submit grad school letter of rec

Everything is handled by the UC Berkeley letter service. Once the letter is ready I will email you my waiver form which basically states that I waive any access rights to the letter. You then simply email your letter and my waiver (both in PDF formats) to letter@berkeley.edu . After that I can contact the letter service and have them forward/submit your letters to all the graduate schools I apply to.

  • Deadline: Early December

How to submit NSF letter of rec

I will have talked to you about this. When the letter is ready I will send a request through NSF which will send you an email with a link/password/username to login and submit the letter.

  • Deadline: November 5th, 5:00 pm (pacific)
